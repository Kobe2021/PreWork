Education


I graduated from Bowie State University in 2021. I received a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Technology. I stayed on campus for freshman year, and junior year. Sophomore and senior year, I commuted. I graduated Cum Laude!


How I got into IT


Approaching senior year of high school, I began to think about what I would like to be doing for a career. I was thinking about every career field, and how it would interest me. Then my mind came to information technology. I began to consider the fact that everything is becoming more and more digital. I began to wonder how devices work, and how devices are able to communicate with one another. At this point I was involved in dual-enrollment. Where I was in high school, but also doing college courses part-time at Prince George's Community College. The dual-enrollment courses I took were networking, hardware, an english course, and an art course. All of which counted as college credits.

In senior year of high school, while taking the networking and hardware courses at PGCC, those courses gave me a basic understanding of how computers talk to one another, how they operate, and the components of a computer. It really sparked my interests. At this point, is when I made the decision that I want to have a career in the IT department.
